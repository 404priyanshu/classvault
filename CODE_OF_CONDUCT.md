# Code Of Conduct

ClassVault welcomes contributors who want to build useful study tools for students.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections quickly.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy and do not share secrets, credentials, or private user data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Publishing vulnerabilities before maintainers have a chance to fix them.
- Spam or off-topic promotion.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and contributions that violate this code.
